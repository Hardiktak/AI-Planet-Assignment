# Algebra — Sequences and Series (AP, GP, HP)

## Arithmetic Progression (AP)

- **General term**: aₙ = a + (n−1)d, where a = first term, d = common difference
- **Sum of n terms**: Sₙ = n/2 · [2a + (n−1)d] = n/2 · (first + last)
- **Arithmetic Mean (AM)** of a and b: AM = (a + b)/2
- If a, b, c are in AP: 2b = a + c

## Geometric Progression (GP)

- **General term**: aₙ = a · r^(n−1), where r = common ratio
- **Sum of n terms**: Sₙ = a(rⁿ − 1)/(r − 1) for r ≠ 1
- **Sum to infinity** (|r| < 1): S∞ = a/(1 − r)
- **Geometric Mean (GM)** of a and b: GM = √(ab)
- If a, b, c are in GP: b² = ac

## Harmonic Progression (HP)

- A sequence is in HP if the reciprocals form an AP
- **Harmonic Mean (HM)** of a and b: HM = 2ab/(a + b)
- **AM ≥ GM ≥ HM** for positive numbers (equality when a = b)

## Arithmetico-Geometric Progression (AGP)

- Each term = (AP term) × (GP term)
- Sum: Sₙ = Σ [a + (k−1)d] · r^(k−1)
- Sum to infinity (|r| < 1): S∞ = a/(1−r) + dr/(1−r)²

## Useful Summation Formulas

- Σk (k=1 to n) = n(n+1)/2
- Σk² = n(n+1)(2n+1)/6
- Σk³ = [n(n+1)/2]²

## JEE Tips

- When three numbers are in AP, take them as a−d, a, a+d.
- When three numbers are in GP, take them as a/r, a, ar.
- Telescoping: if partial fractions give 1/(k·(k+1)) = 1/k − 1/(k+1), most terms cancel.
